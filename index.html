DISCOVER MALAYSIA

ABOUT MALAYSIA

Malaysia is one of those cities that never gets old, a captivating tourist destination that seamlessly blends modern urban centers with rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. No matter how many times you’ve been, you’ll always discover something new and exciting to see, do, and eat.

ITINERARY

01

Arrival Day in Malaysia from Singapore (17 th Aug 2024)

Pickup at 10:00 am from Singapore a 5-hour drive to Ibis Hotel Kuala Lumpur. 3:00 pm Check into the hotel for a relaxed evening at your leisure to recharge and await a day filled with adventure!

02

Kuala Lumpur to Genting and Batu Caves (18 th Aug)

After breakfast, transfer to Genting Highlands, enjoying a scenic 1-hour drive and cable car ride with breathtaking views. We will then visit the impressive Batu Caves to see Hindu statue and climb 272 steps for city views. Savor lunch at a top restaurant, explore the area, then return to the city for evening activities or a relaxing night in.

03

Sunway lagoon Theme Park (19 th Aug)

Begin your day with adventure: enjoy rides and attractions at the Sunway Lagoon Theme Park. The park offers Water, Amusement, Wildlife, Extreme, Scream and Sunway Lost Lagoon Park

04

Kuala Lumpur Cultural City Tour (20th Aug)

Start your day with a tour of Kuala Lumpur, visiting the National Monument, National Mosque, National Museum, Merdeka Square, Batik factory and the King's Palace. Enjoy lunch in KLCC Park, then marvel at the Petronas Twin Towers and Menara KL Tower.3. End the day shopping in China Town and Central Market.

05

Checkout of Ibis (21 st Aug)

Enjoy your breakfast in the morning, check out from the hotel before 12pm. then get picked up from the hotel to be transferred to the airport to catch your flight
